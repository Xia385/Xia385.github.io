 Nice to Meet You.

Hi, I'm Xia, ​a multidisciplinary designer ​with a keen focus on user interface, user interaction, and e-commerce website design, bolstered by a solid foundation in marketing strategy   and data analysis  . 
I aspire to ​blend data-driven insights and marketing strategies with design to  deliver research-validated solutions,​ enhance user experiences, and drive success for employers.

I have recently completed my master's degree in Management Science at the University of Waterloo, with a primary focus on statistical methods and machine learning algorithms for data analysis.

Previously, I worked at TCL in China as Human/Machine Interface Designer and E-Commerce Graphic Designer for 7 years. Over the years, I have ​been​ exploring diverse areas of design, including content marketing design aligned with business objectives, e-commerce graphic design rooted in marketing strategy, and user-centric human-machine interface design. During this time,​ I developed expertise in designing data-driven solutions and collaborating with cross-functional teams.
