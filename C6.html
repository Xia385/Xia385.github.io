TCL C6 Washing Machine ​Human/Computer Interface Design

TCL C6 product is a series of laundry appliances that includes washing machines, dryers, and combination washer-dryers. They feature new program design and the application of colorful film sheets, which can provide care for clothing through technologies such as negative ion sterilization and steam wrinkle removal.

It was developed and designed by a subsidiary of TCL located in Hefei, China. As a member of the project team, my main responsibilities included the design and implementation of the product's user interface and body label indicators.

Type: Group Client Project

Duration : 9 months (Oct. 2021—Jun.2022)

Tools:  CorelDRAW

Key Skills: User Research, ​UI, ​Physical Prototypes, Material Application

Context
Growing market demand

Due to the impact of the pandemic, washing machines with the capability to disinfect and sterilize clothing have garnered the attention of consumers in the Chinese region. According to the 2020 JD.com Laundry Appliance Industry Report, sales of washing machines with antibacterial and mite removal features accounted for 41% of the total, while sales of dryers saw a year-on-year increase of 97%. Consumers are willing to allocate a higher budget to purchase similar products in order to safeguard the health of themselves and their families.

Additionally, within TCL's existing washing machine product line, there is a lack of competitively priced washing machines in the mid-range market segment.

Therefore, in this project, I will focus on the disinfection, mite removal, and drying functionalities of the washing machine, aiming to provide users with a user-friendly and intuitive operational experience while meeting the requirements of production technology and cost to achieve our business objectives.
